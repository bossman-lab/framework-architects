# How Did Stanislaw Lem Imagine Advanced Computer Intelligence?

**Date:** June 2026  
**Source:** [Marginal Revolution](https://marginalrevolution.com/marginalrevolution/2026/06/how-did-stanislaw-lem-imagine-advanced-computer-intelligence.html)

From Lem's *Imaginary Magnitude*, the Golem IV section on how an AGI is likely to behave:

> GOLEM's behavior is unpredictable. Sometimes it converses courteously with people, whereas on other occasions any attempt at contact misfires. GOLEM sometimes cracks jokes, too, though its sense of humor is fundamentally different from man's. Much depends on its interlocutors. In exceptional cases GOLEM will show a certain interest in people who are talented in a particular way; it is intrigued, so to speak, not by mathematical aptitude — not even the greatest — but rather by interdisciplinary forms of talent.
