# The Nationalization of American Science

**Date:** June 2026  
**Source:** [Marginal Revolution](https://marginalrevolution.com/marginalrevolution/2026/06/the-nationalization-of-american-science.html)

Key thesis: OMB and forty grantmaking agencies (NSF, HHS, DOE, NASA, DOD) have proposed a sweeping rewrite of federal grant rules that would shift American science from state-funded but not state-directed to top-down, state control.

### Key Proposed Changes

**Politicization & Centralization:**
- Program goals must be "aligned with administration policies and priorities."
- Merit review downgraded: senior appointees conduct reviews; peer review is only "advisory."
- At-will termination: grants can be terminated at any time if priorities change.
- Loyalty clause: recipients must ensure subrecipients do not harm federal government reputation.

**Increased Bureaucracy:**
- Payment requests require written justifications.
- All recipients must run E-Verify.
- New pre-issuance review committee of senior appointees.

### Analysis

The system's previous virtue: decentralization — inefficient like markets/federalism, but allows experimentation, error correction, robustness. New system: efficient like ministries of science — an experiment with known failure modes.

> "America is moving in the wrong direction. We should double down on what made America great. Instead we are adopting all of the loser policies of authoritarian nations."
