# Sometimes It Is Hard to Solve for the Equilibrium

**Date:** June 2026  
**Source:** [Marginal Revolution](https://marginalrevolution.com/marginalrevolution/2026/06/sometimes-it-is-hard-to-solve-for-the-equilibrium.html)

The US government has issued an export control directive (citing national security authorities) to suspend all access to Anthropic's Fable 5 and Mythos 5 for any foreign national, including foreign national Anthropic employees. As a result, Anthropic must disable these models for all customers. The directive follows reports (not yet confirmed) that the model could be jailbroken.

### Constraints on the US Government

1. **Business viability** — US needs major AI companies to stay in business. Restricting foreign hiring hurts recruitment.
2. **Model access as a tool of power** — USG wants to use model access for hard and soft power.
3. **Competition with China** — US must stay ahead in the AI race.
4. **Enforceability** — "US citizens only" is insufficient.
5. **Nationalization is not viable** — USG cannot nationalize top AI companies.
6. **Open-source competition** — Chinese and other open-source models improve at a reasonable pace.

> "I am willing to reject the philosophy of 'safetyism' and bite various associated bullets. As it stands, these actions will not succeed in making us safer."
