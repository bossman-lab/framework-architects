# The Google Capital Company

**Date:** June 1, 2026  
**Source:** [Stratechery by Ben Thompson](https://stratechery.com/2026/the-google-capital-company/)

> "What does the most beautiful business model of all time look like? First, imagine that your supply is free. Second, imagine that your customers willfully compete against each other to raise your prices. Third, imagine that your users decide which of your customers gets the privilege of paying you."

### The Aggregator Business Model (Google Services)

Characteristics: Supply is free (web content), customers (advertisers) bid against each other, users (searchers) determine which ads are shown.

### Berkshire Hathaway's Capital Recycling Strategy

See's Candies (high margin, low capital needs) generated cash that funded BNSF Railway (low margin, high capital). 2025: Berkshire had $373B cash and $25B free cash flow.

### Google Cloud's Growth Trajectory

Google Cloud growing rapidly: Q1 2026 revenue $20.0B with $6.6B profit. Cloud as % of Services revenue growing from 6% in Q4 2019 to 22% in Q1 2026. Potential to surpass absolute profits of Services.

### The $80 Billion Equity Raise

Structure includes $40B ATM program, $30B underwritten offerings, and $10B from Berkshire Hathaway. Why equity instead of debt? Demand for compute is still underestimated. Berkshire is betting on Google.

### Compute as the Ultimate Commodity

If compute becomes the primary bottleneck, the company with the most cash capacity will win. Berkshire is betting on Google.
