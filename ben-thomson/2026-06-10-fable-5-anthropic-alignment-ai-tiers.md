# Fable 5, Anthropic Alignment, AI Tiers

**Date:** June 10, 2026  
**Source:** [Stratechery by Ben Thompson](https://stratechery.com/2026/fable-5-anthropic-alignment-ai-tiers/)

This Stratechery Plus article discusses Fable 5 (the public version of Mythos), its capabilities, and the troubling precedents it sets. It also explores Anthropic's alignment approach and a proposed tier system for AI.

### Key Points

Fable 5 is the public release of the previously internal AI system Mythos. The model is described as "very capable" but raises concerns about safety, transparency, or control (implied by "troubling precedents"). The article analyzes guardrails implemented in Fable 5.

Anthropic's alignment philosophy is compared to Apple's approach — both prioritize safety and user control, but through different mechanisms. A framework for AI tiers is introduced, categorizing models by capability and risk level.
