# An Interview with Microsoft CEO Satya Nadella About Finding Core Competencies

**Date:** June 4, 2026  
**Source:** [Stratechery by Ben Thompson](https://stratechery.com/2026/an-interview-with-microsoft-ceo-satya-nadella-about-finding-core-competencies/)

Interview conducted after Microsoft Build 2026 keynote. Nadella was the sole presenter (aside from demos), indicating a shift to a more hands-on role.

### Key Quotes

> "Every company thinks they can do everything, and then they realize that the world doesn't need them to, the world wants them to do the one thing."

> "Zune was a great device, but the world didn't need Zune from us, and so that was the end of it."

> "I'm very proud of the fact that we came together… the fact that there is a company now that may go public and be a trillion-dollar company… At the end of the day, we are an operating company, investment is just more of an accident."

> "We now need to take what we built, rebuild it for the agent era and change the levers of the business model such that you have a per-user business model and you have a consumption business model."

> "Can you think of a platform and platform rules… which are built for the agent era? … I want to open that up."

### MAI Models (Microsoft's Seven New Models)

Built from scratch with clean lineage (no distillation during hill-climbing). Goal: create a multi-tenant hill-climbing system where enterprises can create private benchmarks/evals, build their own RL environment, and invite all models to compete on those private evals.

### OpenAI Partnership & Capex

Partnership status: "Until 2032, we still have a lot as a customer of theirs, them as a customer of ours, as an IP partner." Microsoft is an operating company first; the investment in OpenAI was "an accident."

### The Software Business & AI-era Business Models

The future firm will have human capital and token capital. Token capital comes from owning some set of weights (not just API calls). Microsoft's vision is to help cautious enterprises embrace the future on their terms.
